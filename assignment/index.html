SOME OF THE PHOTOS I TOOK

Rudkhan Castle

Located 25km southwest of Fuman city north of Iran in Gilan province, it is a military complex which was constructed during the Sassanian era (224-651), and later rebuilt in 1096 by the Nizari Isma'ilis for use by the Assassins. The castle is built on two tips of a mount, with an area of 2.6 hectares (6.4 acres). Its architects made use of natural mountainous features in the construction of the fort.

Kura Dawè

In the last days of autumn, a trip to Kura Dawè village, Kura Dawè village is located of Mawat district in Sharbazher and about 50 km away from Sulaimani city. In the winter there are nearly 25 houses that live in this village, and in the summer there are nearly 40 houses live in Kura Dawè village.

National Museum Amnasuraka

From 1979 to 1991, during Saddam Hussein's rule in Iraq, Amna Suraka was the northern headquarters of the Mukhabarat, Iraq's intelligence agency. Many people were imprisoned there, especially students, Kurdish nationalists, and other dissidents. Many were tortured and raped. The prison was captured by Peshmerga forces during the 1991 uprisings in Iraq; 800 Iraqi soldiers retreated to the prison and all were killed by Kurdish forces. The building has many bullet marks from that battle.

Galata Kulesi

Yaklaşık 63 metre yüksekliği ile İstanbul, Türkiye'nin en yüksek binalarından biridir ve dokuz kattan oluşmaktadır. Bu kule uzun zamandır şehrin en eski yapısı ve şehrin en yüksek noktası olarak kabul edildi, ancak şimdi birçok yüksek bina inşa edildi. 1349 yılında şehrin savunma duvarının bir parçası olarak tamamlanan Galata Kulesi, Konstantinopolis antik kentini korumak için inşa edilmiş ve antik kitaplarda İsa'nın Kulesi olarak anılıyor. Bu yüksek kule, çevredeki limanları izlemek ve kontrol etmek için hazırlanmıştır.

Kish Island

Island in Iran Kish is a 91.5-square-kilometre resort island in Bandar Lengeh County, Hormozgān Province, off the southern coast of Iran in the Persian Gulf. Owing to its free trade zone status, the island is touted as a consumer's paradise, with numerous malls, shopping centres, tourist attractions, and resort hotels.

Anzali Lagoon

Anzali lagoon is on the coast of the Caspian Sea, near the port of Bandar-e Anzali. The local people call it Talab-e -e or Mordab-e Anzali. But Russian call it Enzeliiskii or Pekhleviiskii zaliv. The lagoon divides Port Anzali into two parts; Selke Wildlife Refuge and the Siahkesheem Marsh. It holds a bunch of islands and hosts of hundreds of different birds, animals and plant species. It is the most unique lagoon in the country cause of type and humidity. The Talab was once one of the biggest fishing areas of the country.
